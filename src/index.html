Welcome to Hashminer!

Hashminer is an interactive raffle game that runs on the Ethereum Blockchain.

There are no house fees and the only cost to play are blockchain transaction fees

To play:

  1. Clicking on a valid area on the map and confirm the transaction on MetaMask to join game
  3. Once the game is full, and 3 blocks have been added to the Ethereum Blockchain, any player can reveal the winner by clicking on the "Reveal Winner" button
